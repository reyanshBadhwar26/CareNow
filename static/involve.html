How Your Contribution Helps

The impact of community participation

1

You Report a Wait Time

After visiting a clinic, you submit a report with your check-in time, check-out time, and the clinic's condition.

2

Data is Processed

Our AI system processes your report along with others, updating clinic statistics and improving prediction models.

3

Predictions Improve

With more data, our machine learning model becomes more accurate, providing better wait time predictions.

4

Patients Benefit

Other patients can now make informed decisions about which clinic to visit, saving time and reducing stress.
